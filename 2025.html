Moodboard Description

This moodboard is inspired by one of my dnd characters, a lunar sorcerer ‘’tiefling’’ (shes actually a homebrew race and fell from the sky as an infant, but doesn't know this). Her vibes are entirely exhausted college student in need of a nap and coffee, who only wants to be comfy and doing research or reading at all times. Her design is loosely sheep inspired, with very warm shades of brown and other muted colors being the colors associated with her and her vibes.

Moodboard Description

‘A blindfold wraps over your eyes, growing tighter and tighter. All you see is the haze of red that you can't escape.’
